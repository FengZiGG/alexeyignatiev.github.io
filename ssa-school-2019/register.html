Registration

Registration at the summer school is free of charge but mandatory for all attendees. Registration deadline is May 31 June 10, 2019.

Please, take into consideration that the number of attendees is limited. Register as soon as you are sure to be able to attend the summer school.

Note that scholarships will be available for students enrolled in US universities, thanks to the NSF support. Undergraduate, master, and PhD students are eligible. The results will be announced in June.
